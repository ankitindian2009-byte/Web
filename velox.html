Founded in 2019 by a collective of aerospace engineers, Formula 1 aerodynamicists, and mastercraft artisans, VELOX Automobiles emerged from a single, uncompromising conviction: that the ultimate driving machine had not yet been built.

Every VELOX is handcrafted at our atelier in Zurich, Switzerland. Each vehicle is bespoke — unique in specification, unique in soul. The machinery is aerospace. The soul is art.
